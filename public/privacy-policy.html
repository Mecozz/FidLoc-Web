Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the FidLoc web application and the FidLoc Forms Filler Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

FidLoc Web App:

  • Account information (email address) for authentication
  • Location data you choose to save (equipment locations, addresses, coordinates)
  • Activity logs for team collaboration features

FidLoc Forms Filler Extension:

  • User preferences stored locally in your browser (technician name, garage selection, equipment status)
  • Serial number queue stored locally in your browser

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the FidLoc service
  • To enable team collaboration and location sharing
  • To save your preferences for form filling

Data Storage

FidLoc Web App: Data is stored securely in Google Firebase servers.

Chrome Extension: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data from the extension is transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Location data you save in FidLoc is shared only with members of your organization/team as configured by your team administrator.

Chrome Extension Permissions

The FidLoc Forms Filler extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with Microsoft Forms when you click the fill button
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • scripting: To fill form fields on Microsoft Forms pages
  • Host permissions (forms.office.com): To detect and interact with Microsoft Forms

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. The web app uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules. The Chrome extension stores data only on your local device.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the FidLoc app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Clear extension data through Chrome's extension settings

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us through the FidLoc app or GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
